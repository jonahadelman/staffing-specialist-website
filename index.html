The Human Behind The Work

There can only be one.

I started The Staffing Specialist at a point in my career where I had already achieved everything I thought I wanted.

I had built and sold two companies, worked with top executives including Jack and Suzy Welch, found success in an industry I loved, and could have continued on that path. Instead, I made a different decision.

I wanted to get back to the ground floor, inside a company, where I could just be a recruiter. Not a CEO, not removed from the work. Just a recruiter. Okay, maybe "the recruiter."

What I didn't expect was what came next. I found a completely new level of energy, purpose, and love for what I do.

"When I partner with a company, I'm all in. I want to feel like the company is mine, because that's when the work is at its best."

I don't operate on contingency. I don't believe in retained search models where there's distance from the outcome. When you get that version of me, you get a recruiter who will not stop until the role is filled — and filled with someone who makes the company better.

No transactions. No fee-per-hire. Just a recruiter on a mission, with a purpose to be part of something special.

My success has never come from tactics alone. It comes from genuinely caring: about the company, about the people, about the mission we're all building toward.

Over the last 25 years, I've walked the halls and seen the faces of people I've hired — people who are now leading teams, driving innovation, and shaping the future of the business.

That's the real ROI. That's the part I'm most proud of.

If you ever have the chance to work with me, you will know very quickly that I am the recruiter who truly never stops recruiting.

Grateful.
